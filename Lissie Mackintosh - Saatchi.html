Zac McDOnald is a Presenter and Content Creator

Quickly emerging as one of the rising stars in motorsport broadcasting, Zac McDonald is a Sky Sports and Formula 1 presenter, as well as a competitive karting driver. Known for his passion and dedication to racing, Zac brings fresh energy to Formula 1 coverage, regularly engaging fans through insightful interviews and behind-the-scenes content.

He works closely with Formula 1 teams, partners, and motorsport manufacturers as a presenter and live events host.

Zac has been a prominent figure in the Formula 1 paddock, conducting interviews with some of the sport's most iconic figures, including Lewis Hamilton, Sebastian Vettel, Toto Wolff, Stefano Domenicali, Lando Norris, and Zak Brown.

Zac is actively building a growing social media following, particularly on Instagram and YouTube, where he offers fans an exclusive behind-the-scenes look into his life both on and off the track. His engaging content frequently includes his interactions with key figures from the motorsport and entertainment industries.

Zac also presents on F1 Kids, a show that reaches 9 territories globally, where he helps introduce the next generation of fans to the world of Formula 1 with fun, informative content.

With a unique position as both a racing driver and presenter, Zac possesses an impressive depth of knowledge in motorsport that sets him apart. His expertise has proven invaluable for brands seeking authentic engagement with motorsport audiences.

Zac plays a pivotal role in live hosting events such as Formula 1 car launches and major motorsport awards ceremonies.
